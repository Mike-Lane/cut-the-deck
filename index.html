About

We wanted to make an app where you can play card games when you have time to spare or just get your mind off things. So far we have Black Jack and a Mermory Match Game that you can choose from.

Black Jack

Your goal is to draw cards with a value as close to 21 as possible without going over. A hand that goes over 21 is a bust or break . The players at a blackjack table do not play against each other, they play against the dealer. Each player only has to beat the dealer's hand to Win. You do this in one of two ways: 1 Have a card total value greater than that of the dealer and not "bust". 2 Win “by default” if the dealer “busts.

Memory Match Game

This game is a great family card game. ... When a player turns over two cards that do not match, those cards are turned face down again (in the same position) and it becomes the next player's turn. The trick is to remember which cards are where. The person with the most pairs at the end of the game wins.
